# Feature Requests

Capabilities the owner has asked for that don't exist yet. Appended by the `self-improvement` skill when the agent identifies a gap. Dreaming reads this to track demand patterns — recurring requests get higher priority.

**Entry format**:

```markdown
## [REQ-YYYYMMDD-XXX] short-name

**Logged**: ISO-8601 timestamp
**Priority**: low | medium | high
**Status**: open | in_progress | shipped | declined

### Summary
What the owner wants.

### Use Case
Why they want it, what scenario it serves.

### Notes
Constraints, related skills, existing workarounds.

---
```
